Starting at an early age, SpinZee burst into the Nottingham scene back in 2000. Due to her unique eclectic music style, SpinZee soon built up an awesome reputation thanks to her feisty musical selection combined with her flawless technical prowess.

Her skills brought her to the attention of Unity Promotions, an Asian based DJ agency run by hip hop guru DJ DSK.

During 2006 - 2008 SpinZee toured over 60 cities in Asia, headlining events sponsored by Budweiser, Chivas, Johnnie Walker and Smirnoff.

Her numerous residences and guest appearances allowed her to play along side world renowned artist such as Orbital, Freestylers, Fergie, Josh Wink, Freq Nasty, Ben Simms, Audio Bullies, Rennie Pilgrem, Plump DJs, Jon Carter, Erol Alkan, DJ Marky, Scratch Perverts, Dj Vadim, Mr Thing, Plus One, Prime Cuts, DJ First Rate. to name but a few.

By 2009, SpinZee returned to the UK to reside in Portsmouth. A lack of venues in this seaside town, caused SpinZee to turn to her mates and say.......

"F&#K it, who wants to go to the beach?" and with that she built herself a Rave Buggy.

For the next few years, SpinZee continued with her love of DJing by hosting mini raves on the beach with her portable PA system, the Rave Buggy.

In 2013 a new radio station Lush 97.9 FM kicked off. For the next few years, SpinZee hosted a weekly show spinning the latest garage, drum and bass, and hip hop. Her shows quickly became well known due to her killer bass selections teamed with her cheeky presenting skills.

In 2014, SpinZee had a baby girl. She continued with her weekly radio show whilst on maternity leave, but took a break from spinning in night clubs, whilst she concentrated on motherhood.

In 2015, Lynchpins of the south coast’s drum and bass scene from past and present came together to form Ohm. Bringing Dnb heavy weights Friction, Break, Randall, Alix Perez, Skeptical, Ulterior Motive and Commix via superb, high production parties along the South Coast.

This brings us to present day....SpinZee has secured a handful of gigs....and the return of Ohm to Portsmouth coming very soon.........

SpinZee mixes, scratches, cuts, and generally rips up the dance floor with the heaviest breaks sounds imaginable. A DJ that will really wow the crowd.

She's always on the hunt to play at club nights & radio events hosted by fun, like minded DnB headz...Just get in touch....She won't bite...only scratch :)
